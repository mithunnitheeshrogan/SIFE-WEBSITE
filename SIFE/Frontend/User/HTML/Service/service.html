Building Fire Safety System

A Building Fire Safety System is a comprehensive setup designed to detect, prevent, and respond to fire hazards within a structure. It typically includes smoke detectors, fire alarms, sprinklers, and emergency response protocols. Modern systems may also integrate smart technologies for real-time monitoring and automated alerts, ensuring the safety of occupants and minimizing property damage.

OEM & System Integration

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)

OEMs manufacture components or products that are purchased by another company and retailed under that purchasing company’s brand name. For example, a company that produces hard drives used in a branded laptop.

System Integration

System Integration is the process of linking together different computing systems and software applications physically or functionally, to act as a coordinated whole—like combining sensors, control units, and software into a single solution.
